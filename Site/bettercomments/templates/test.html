About Project 13

Project 13 started in the spring of 2012 in the course "Collaboration in Journalism and Technology" at Northwestern University. This class brought together two computer science and two journalism students together to create Refine in ten weeks. Project 13 is Alex Madjar, Josiah Matlack, Marisa Paulson, and Ali Pisano. Find out more about them.

Where are we?

Project 13 is a joint project between Northwestern University's McCormick School of Engineering and Medill School of Journalism. We're based in Evanston, IL.
